Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our loan products

Credit score requirements vary by loan type. Personal loans typically require a minimum score of 620, while mortgage loans may require higher scores depending on the program. We evaluate your entire financial profile, not just your credit score, so even if your score is below our typical requirements, you may still qualify.

For personal and auto loans, many applicants receive an instant decision online. Final approval and funding typically occurs within 1-2 business days after we receive all required documentation. Mortgage loans take longer due to property appraisal and underwriting requirements, usually 30-45 days from application to closing.

No, RamzBank does not charge prepayment penalties on any of our loan products. You can pay off your loan early at any time without incurring additional fees. In fact, paying off your loan early may save you money on interest.

Required documents vary by loan type. For most loans, you'll need:

  • Government-issued ID
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements)
  • Proof of residence
  • Social Security number
  • For mortgages: additional documentation about the property and your financial history

Yes, we offer refinancing options for auto loans, personal loans, and mortgages. Refinancing may help you secure a lower interest rate, reduce your monthly payment, or change your loan term. Our loan officers can review your current loan terms and help determine if refinancing makes financial sense for your situation.
