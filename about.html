About RamzBank

Trusted financial services for individuals and businesses since 1985

Our Story

Founded in 1985, RamzBank began as a small community bank with a simple mission: to help people achieve their financial goals. What started as a single branch in downtown has grown into one of the nation's most trusted financial institutions.

Through decades of economic changes, we've remained committed to our core values of integrity, innovation, and exceptional customer service. Today, we serve over 2 million customers nationwide with a full range of banking services.

Our growth has always been guided by the needs of our customers. From introducing online banking in 1999 to launching our award-winning mobile app, we've consistently been at the forefront of banking technology while maintaining the personal touch that our customers value.
