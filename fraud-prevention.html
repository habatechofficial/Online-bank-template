Fraud Prevention

Protect yourself from financial fraud with our expert security tips

Common Fraud Types

  • Phishing emails pretending to be from your bank
  • Fake tech support calls requesting remote access
  • Investment scams with "guaranteed" high returns

Protection Tips

  • Never share passwords or OTPs with anyone
  • Verify website URLs before entering credentials
  • Enable two-factor authentication
